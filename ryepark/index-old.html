The Ripe Ark

Steve Moss and Lesley Mosbey - Our house at Rye Park NSW


In August 2007 we bought this old slab cottage on one acre in Rye Park, which is near Boorowa north of Yass. It was pretty run-down, but structurally sound and not in need of any major renovations. We moved here in April 2009, having finally been able to wind up our affairs in Sydney. We are very happy to be living in Rye Park! Don't forget to scroll down to the latest news.
